Frequently Asked Questions

Why I should choose your company?

Never Clog Gutters is a local owned and operated company. We’re not some corporate giant who doesn’t care about you and your home. We don’t cut corners and we don’t price gouge which has become all too regular in the big corporate world of gutter guards.

How do gutter guards work?

Think of gutter guards like a filter over top of your gutters. Leaves, dirt, and other debris is kept out, while the water gets through the filter and into your gutters just like normal.

How much do gutter guards cost?

Gutter guards are priced by linear foot as well as a few other factors that differ for each house. We’re transparent with all our pricing so you know exactly how much the work is going to cost before we start. There are no extra charges that ‘mysteriously’ come up after we start working.
